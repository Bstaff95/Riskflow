# Reset Depth Watch

The practice of classifying how deeply a hot oscillator cools before it becomes actionable again.

## Reset Depths

- shallow: holds near the prior pre-launch oscillator base
- medium: cools toward [[Zero Line Support]]
- deep: returns to [[Minus Two Retest Hold]] or [[Minus One Point Five Retest Hold]]

## Role In The Grammar

Reset depth helps separate:

- healthy leader reset
- delayed continuation
- full failure / distribution
