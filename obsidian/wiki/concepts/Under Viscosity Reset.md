# Under Viscosity Reset

The oscillator rolls below viscosity after a strong move.

## Role In The Grammar

This often marks a shift from active momentum to reset/watch mode. It should be read differently depending on context:

- after a hot impulse: possible healthy reset
- after weak chop: possible failed strength
- in deep negative zones: possible continued weakness unless weakness fails to accelerate
