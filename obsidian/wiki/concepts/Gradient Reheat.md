# Gradient Reheat

The oscillator begins changing color hotter again after a reset or base.

## Role In The Grammar

Gradient reheat is a possible early clue that reset is ending before the oscillator fully confirms through a major level.

It is especially useful after:

- [[Hot Leader Cooloff]]
- [[Reset Depth Watch]]
- [[Failed Breakdown]]
- [[Viscosity Retest Hold]]
- [[Lower Band Support]]

## Review Questions

- Does reheat happen before price expansion?
- Is the reheat broad and sustained or only a one-bar impulse?
- Does reheat work better after compression, lower-band support, or viscosity reclaim?
