# Break Below Plus One Point Five Reset

The oscillator falls below `+1.5` after previously reaching a hot leader zone.

## Role In The Grammar

This can mark a meaningful cooling phase. It is not automatically bearish, but it usually means the easy momentum leg is no longer early.
