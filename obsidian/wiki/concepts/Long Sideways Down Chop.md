# Long Sideways Down Chop

A long oscillator structure where the signal moves sideways to downward, often below zero or near the lower bands.

## Role In The Grammar

This can be an accumulation or exhaustion structure when weakness stops accelerating. It is more useful when followed by a clean retest, trendline break, viscosity reclaim, or gradient reheat.
