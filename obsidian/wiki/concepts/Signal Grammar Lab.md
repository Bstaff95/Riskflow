the grammar research without jumping across unrelated ideas.

## Policy

- Keep the base oscillator frozen for now.
- Treat pressure waves as a candidate family, not the mission.
- Do not tune weights, z-score lookback, viscosity, or gradient until the grammar has evidence.
- Use memes for discovery, then validate on broader crypto and equities.

## Product Direction

The eventual product should feel like a universal adaptive relative chart indicator. A user should be able to compare an asset to a benchmark or basket and quickly understand:

- is the asset improving?
- is it likely to outperform?
- is it early, ready, weak, or exhausted?
- is the visual setup confirmed or still only a watchlist idea?
