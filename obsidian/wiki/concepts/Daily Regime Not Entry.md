# Daily Regime Not Entry

The daily oscillator identifies a regime/watchlist shift, but does not provide a precise execution trigger by itself.

## Role In The Grammar

Daily can answer:

- is the asset becoming worth attention?
- is the prior weakness stalling?
- is the broad reversal structure improving?

Lower timeframes may still be needed to answer:

- where is the actual trigger?
- did the reclaim retest cleanly?
- is the setup compressed enough for entry?
