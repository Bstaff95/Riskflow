# PEPE 1D Human Review - Divergence Sequence

## Human Read

The generated review point was after price had already pumped, so the better read is to move backward and inspect the structure before and after the pump.

What mattered:

- price came off a capitulatory low
- signal rejected near the zero line
- price then went higher
- price made a higher high while the signal made a lower high
- that created bearish divergence
- price later double bottomed
- during the double bottom, the oscillator made higher lows
- that bullish divergence led into another price leg higher

## Principle

The oscillator can be used for both bearish and bullish divergence. A post-pump zero rejection plus lower oscillator high can warn of weakness, while a later price double bottom with higher oscillator lows can mark renewed accumulation.

```text
price higher high + oscillator lower high near/under zero = bearish divergence warning
price double bottom + oscillator higher lows = bullish divergence continuation/reversal
```
