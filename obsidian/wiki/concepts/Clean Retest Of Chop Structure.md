# Clean Retest Of Chop Structure

After a long oscillator chop, the signal returns to test the prior chop or breakout structure and holds.

## Role In The Grammar

This can confirm that the old weakness structure is turning into support. On daily, it may be a regime signal rather than an entry trigger.
